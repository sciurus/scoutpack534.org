Cub Scout Pack 534 serves students and families at Sagamore Hills Elementary, Hawthorne Elementary, Annunciation Day School, Shallowford Presbyterian School, and members of Shallowford Presbyterian Church, but others are welcome to join. The Pack is a family pack, welcoming boys and girls in grades K - 5th. We meet at Shallowford Presbyterian Church in Atlanta, GA. Pack members cross into Troop 534.

Starting as early as Kindergarten, Pack 534 provide opportunites for the entire family to participate in day trips, overnight camping, and hand-on activities such as archery, civics, crafts, pioneering, and stem activities.

Pack 534 holds Den Meetings every Tuesday night from 7 - 8pm. The last Tuesday of the month is the Pack Meeting where Cub Scouts are awarded belt loops, badges, and pins for completing Adventures toward rank.

Students can enroll at any time of year and participate as much or little as their schedules allow.

We typically meet in the Great Hall and classrooms inside Shallowford Presbyterian Church, 2375 Shallowford Road NE, Atlanta, GA 30345

For enrollment information or any questions, please email scoutpack534@gmail.com
